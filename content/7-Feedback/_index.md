### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is professional and friendly. The open atmosphere makes it easy for me to communicate and learn from others. Clear workflows and sufficient supporting tools help me maintain productivity and stay focused on my tasks.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor team is very supportive of everyone. Whenever I encounter difficulties, I always receive detailed guidance and suggestions on how to resolve the issues. The team encourages me to research proactively, which has helped me improve my self-learning and problem-solving skills.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned are aligned with my academic major, allowing me to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world projects. I also have the opportunity to explore new technologies that are not covered in the university curriculum.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I gained a great deal of knowledge about Cloud, including deployment processes, resource management, and essential services within the cloud ecosystem. These skills have helped me build a solid foundation for my future career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
During my internship, the company organized many events where I had the chance to listen to real experiences and insights shared by senior employees in the industry. These sharing sessions provided valuable knowledge and helped me better understand potential career paths.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company’s policies are clear and were communicated from the beginning. I always understood the expectations throughout the internship period. The company provided a supportive environment, giving me access to the necessary resources to complete my tasks efficiently.

### Additional Questions

**What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
I was most satisfied with the opportunity to participate in events about Cloud, GenAI, and DevOps, and to listen to valuable sharing from experienced professionals. These experiences helped broaden my knowledge and gave me new perspectives for my future career.

**What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
The company could organize more team bonding activities or workshops for interns to connect and learn from each other. More structured onboarding or training at the beginning would also help new interns adapt faster.

**If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
Yes, I would recommend this internship to my friends because the environment is friendly, the mentors are dedicated, and there are many opportunities to learn and grow both professionally and personally.

### Suggestions & Expectations

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
Regular one-on-one check-ins or feedback meetings (weekly or bi-weekly) would allow interns to better understand their progress and identify areas for improvement.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
Yes, I would be happy to continue if given the opportunity, as I feel there is still much to learn and contribute.

**Any other comments (free sharing):**  
Thank you to everyone in the team for your support and guidance throughout my internship. I truly appreciate the experience and hope to stay connected in the future.
